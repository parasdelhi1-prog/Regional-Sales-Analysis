## 🎯 Project Overview

I completed a comprehensive **Regional Sales Analysis** project, leveraging five years of U.S. sales data to uncover regional trends, product performance, and profitability drivers. The project combined **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** with a **Power BI dashboard**, delivering actionable insights for sales teams and decision makers.

This is an **end-to-end analytics project** showcasing real-world data science skills.

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

### 1. **Seasonality Patterns**
- Sales **peak in May–June**
- Notable dips in **January and April**
- *Implication:* Launch seasonal promotions to smooth revenue

### 2. **SKU Concentration Risk**
- Products **26 & 25 contribute ~25%** of total sales
- Heavy reliance on few top performers
- *Implication:* Strengthen mid-tier products to reduce risk

### 3. **Channel Dynamics**
- **Wholesale:** 54% of volume (lower margins)
- **Export:** 38% margins (highest profitability)
- **Direct:** Growing but smaller segment
- *Implication:* Expand Export partnerships for profitability

### 4. **Regional Performance Gap**
- **California:** $230M revenue (market leader)
- **Northeast & Midwest:** Untapped growth potential
- Regional disparities indicate expansion opportunities
- *Implication:* Targeted investment in underperforming regions

### 5. **Customer Segmentation Insights**
- **Large clients:** Low margins → Require discount review
- **Mid-size clients:** Strong margins → Present upsell opportunities
- **Small clients:** Variable performance → Need tiered strategy
- *Implication:* Segment-based pricing and sales strategies

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1. **Revenue Smoothing**
   - Launch seasonal promotions to balance May–June peaks and January/April dips

2. **Portfolio Optimization**
   - Strengthen mid-tier SKUs
   - Review low-margin items for discontinuation or repricing

3. **Channel Expansion**
   - Expand Export partnerships to capitalize on 38% margins
   - Develop Export-focused account management

4. **Geographic Growth**
   - Invest in Northeast and Midwest markets
   - Develop region-specific sales strategies

5. **Margin Management**
   - Monitor margins closely with alerts
   - Flag and address low-profit orders
   - Implement tiered pricing by customer segment
